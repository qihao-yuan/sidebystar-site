关于我们 About Us

循星科技（杭州）有限公司成立于2025年5月，专注AI 赋能的居家服务（AI2Home）。我们致力于研发生产家居智慧数字大脑及配套终端，构建完善AI2Home 服务生态，为万千家庭带来智能、便捷、安全的居家新体验。通过创新的家居MBTI测试系统，我们为每个用户提供个性化的智能家居解决方案。 SidebyStar (Hangzhou) Co., Ltd. was established in May 2025, focusing on AI-empowered home services (AI2Home). We are committed to the research, development, and production of smart home digital brains and supporting terminals, building a comprehensive AI2Home service ecosystem to bring intelligent, convenient, and secure new home experiences to thousands of households. Through our innovative Home MBTI testing system, we provide personalized smart home solutions for every user.

我们的核心团队汇聚了海内外顶尖学府的精英人才，并掌握着丰富的产业链资源。团队深度融合人工智能与智能终端技术，兼具前沿核心技术研发实力与大规模量产经验，具备端边云一体化的综合能力。我们开创性地将传统文化与现代科技结合，基于四象理念（玄武、青龙、白虎、朱雀）构建了独特的16型家居人格测试系统，让AI真正理解每个用户的个性化需求。 Our core team brings together elite talents from top universities at home and abroad, possessing rich industry chain resources. The team deeply integrates artificial intelligence and smart terminal technology, boasting both cutting-edge core technology R&D capabilities and large-scale mass production experience, with comprehensive edge-cloud integration capabilities. We innovatively combine traditional culture with modern technology, building a unique 16-type home personality testing system based on the Four Symbols philosophy (Xuanwu, Qinglong, Baihu, Zhuque), enabling AI to truly understand each user's personalized needs.

我们的使命是以创新科技驱动，解决居家生活中的实际痛点，为每个家庭打造个性化、智能化的居家生态系统。通过星璇所感的毫秒级感知、望舒所循的智能学习、曜衡所断的决策引擎，以及丰富的插件生态，我们让"数据主权归用户所有"的理念真正落地，构建真正懂你的智能家居管家。 Our mission is to drive innovation with technology, solve real pain points in home life, and create personalized, intelligent home ecosystems for every family. Through Xinxuan Sensing's millisecond-level perception, Wangshu Learning's intelligent adaptation, Yaoheng Decision's smart engine, and our rich plugin ecosystem, we make the principle of "data sovereignty belongs to users" truly actionable, building intelligent home assistants that truly understand you.
