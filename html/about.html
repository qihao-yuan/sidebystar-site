关于我们 About Us

循星科技（杭州）有限公司成立于2025年5月，专注于 AI 赋能的居家服务（AI2Home）。我们致力于研发生产家居智慧数字大脑及配套终端，构建完善的 AI2Home 服务生态，为万千家庭带来智能、便捷、安全的居家新体验。 SidebyStar (Hangzhou) Co., Ltd. was established in May 2025, focusing on AI-empowered home services (AI2Home). We are committed to the research, development, and production of smart home digital brains and supporting terminals, building a comprehensive AI2Home service ecosystem to bring intelligent, convenient, and secure new home experiences to thousands of households.

我们的核心团队汇聚了海内外顶尖学府的精英人才，并掌握着丰富的产业链资源。团队深度融合人工智能与智能终端技术，兼具前沿核心技术研发实力与大规模量产经验，具备端边云一体化的综合能力。此外，我们在将 AI 技术灵活应用于多种形态智能终端方面，也积累了丰富的个性化实践经验。 Our core team brings together elite talents from top universities at home and abroad, possessing rich industry chain resources. The team deeply integrates artificial intelligence and smart terminal technology, boasting both cutting-edge core technology R&D capabilities and large-scale mass production experience, with comprehensive edge-cloud integration capabilities. Furthermore, we have accumulated extensive personalized practical experience in flexibly applying AI technology to various forms of smart terminals.

我们的使命是以创新科技驱动，解决居家生活中的实际痛点，为消费者打造高效、智能、全方位，无障碍的生态居家解决方案。 Our mission is to drive innovation with technology, solve real pain points in home life, and create efficient, intelligent, comprehensive, and accessible ecological home solutions for consumers.

我们的愿景 Our Vision

我们构建的不只是中控平台，而是有智能、有温度、有理解，有陪伴的智能家庭管家。 We are building more than just a central control platform; we are creating an intelligent family steward with smarts, warmth, understanding, and companionship.

我们致力于打造全场景无障碍、轻松上手的友好交互 We are committed to creating friendly interactions that are accessible in all scenarios and easy to use.

我们要让真正的第六代住宅成为现实，让智慧服务生活，让家成为每个人最舒适的港湾。 We aim to make the true sixth-generation residence a reality, letting intelligence serve life and making home the most comfortable haven for everyone.
