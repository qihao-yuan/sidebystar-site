公司定位 Company Positioning

循星科技 - 私域 AI 操作系统创新者 SidebyStar - Private Domain AI Operating System Innovator

循星科技（杭州）有限公司成立于 2025 年 6 月，技术团队成员均来自北大、新国立、港大、华威大、北邮、西北工大、吉大等国内外高等院校，专注计算数学、大数据、神经网络、统计学、最优化等方向，曾参与各类竞赛及参加多项课题组研究，具备扎实的算法研发能力和硬件适配经验。在循星科技，每一个代码原型都是对研究的延伸更是对学术技术前沿的融合性探索。我们擅长在学术与工程之间来回穿梭，在多模态认知、数据主权、边端部署等方向上持续应用最新学术成果，探索最优迭代路径。 SidebyStar (Hangzhou) Co., Ltd. was established in June 2025. Our technical team members come from top universities including Peking University, National University of Singapore, University of Hong Kong, University of Warwick, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, Northwestern Polytechnical University, Jilin University, and others. We specialize in computational mathematics, big data, neural networks, statistics, optimization, and other fields. Our team has participated in various competitions and multiple research projects, possessing solid algorithm R&D capabilities and hardware adaptation experience. At SidebyStar, every code prototype is an extension of research and an explorative fusion with cutting-edge academic technology. We excel at shuttling between academia and engineering, continuously applying the latest academic achievements in multimodal cognition, data sovereignty, edge deployment, and other directions, exploring optimal iteration paths.

目前团队专注于 AI 赋能的居家服务。我们致力于研发生产家居智慧数字大脑及配套终端，构建完善 AI2Home 服务生态，我们以"数据本地化、算法端侧化、决策自治化"为核心价值，突破传统云依赖模式，为用户带来可信、便捷、安全的智慧生活体验。 Currently, our team focuses on AI-empowered home services. We are committed to developing and producing smart home digital brains and supporting terminals, building a comprehensive AI2Home service ecosystem. With "data localization, algorithm edge deployment, and decision autonomization" as our core values, we break through traditional cloud dependency models to bring users trustworthy, convenient, and secure smart living experiences.

公司愿景 Company Vision

做Web2.5时代数据主权倡导者
让每一个空间都拥有专属的数字灵魂 Data Sovereignty Advocate in Web2.5 Era
Every Space Has Its Own Digital Soul
循星科技致力于： SidebyStar Technology is committed to:

1. 让 AI 走下云端，把算力与智能带回用户身边，数据不出门，隐私不裸奔； Bring AI down from the cloud, return computing power and intelligence to users, keep data local, protect privacy;

2. 让系统懂你所需，通过原型记忆图谱和自适应学习，塑造个性化的数字管家； Make systems understand your needs through prototype memory mapping and adaptive learning, creating personalized digital stewards;

3. 让技术普惠大众，以人为本，循星而动，服务行业解决问题降本增效，也服务个人生活便利与数据自治； Make technology benefit everyone, human-centered, star-guided, serving industries to solve problems and improve efficiency, while serving personal convenience and data autonomy;

4. 让万物协同生长，构建开放插件生态社区，连接家电、传感器、新能源、可穿戴设备等，共同迈向 Web 2.5 私域智能时代。 Enable collaborative growth of all things, build open plugin ecosystem communities, connecting home appliances, sensors, new energy, wearable devices, and more, together advancing toward the Web 2.5 private domain intelligence era.
