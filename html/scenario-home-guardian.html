场景方案SCENARIO SOLUTION

让独居不再孤立，让关怀有据可依Independent Living with Connected Care

面向独居长者与家庭关怀场景，提供异常行为识别、远程关怀通知、本地隐私保护的居家安全方案。让家人安心，让长者有尊严地生活。 Home safety solutions for elderly living alone with anomaly detection, remote care notifications, and local privacy protection. Peace of mind for families, dignity for elders.

适用客户Target Customers

独居长者家庭Elderly Living Alone

子女不在身边的独居老人，需要非侵入式的安全守护。Seniors living alone whose children are away, needing non-intrusive safety monitoring.

家庭关怀服务商Family Care Providers

提供居家照护服务的机构，需要远程+本地结合的监护工具。Home care service providers needing combined remote + local monitoring tools.

社区养老服务Community Elderly Services

社区层面的长者关怀项目，需要批量化的居家守护方案。Community-level elderly care programs needing scalable home guardian solutions.

典型痛点Key Pain Points

  • 独居长者在家发生意外（跌倒、晕厥）无人及时发现Accidents at home (falls, fainting) of elderly living alone go unnoticed
  • 子女远在外地，无法实时了解父母日常状态Children live far away, unable to know parents' daily status in real-time
  • 传统摄像头监控侵犯隐私，长者抗拒使用Traditional camera monitoring invades privacy, rejected by elders
  • 现有穿戴设备依从性差，经常忘记佩戴或充电Poor compliance with wearables -- often forgotten or uncharged

核心功能Core Features

  • 异常行为识别 -- 长时间静止、活动量骤降、异常出入模式自动检测Anomaly detection -- prolonged stillness, sudden activity drops, unusual entry/exit patterns
  • 远程关怀通知 -- 异常事件自动推送至家属/服务人员，支持分级告警Remote care notifications -- auto-push anomalies to family/caregivers with graded alerts
  • 非侵入式感知 -- 毫米波雷达+环境传感器为主，无需摄像头，保护隐私Non-intrusive sensing -- primarily mmWave radar + environmental sensors, no cameras, privacy protected
  • 无穿戴依赖 -- 空间感知替代穿戴设备，无依从性问题No wearable dependency -- spatial sensing replaces wearables, no compliance issues
  • 本地处理 -- 所有数据在本地处理，仅脱敏状态摘要可选上报Local processing -- all data processed locally, only anonymized status summaries optionally reported

交付与实施Delivery & Implementation

部署模式Deployment

本地部署，即插即用。感知节点+本地主机+家属APP。Local deployment, plug-and-play. Sensing nodes + local host + family app.

实施周期Timeline

单户安装：1-2天；社区批量部署：按户数排期。Single household: 1-2 days; Community batch: scheduled by unit count.

可选模块Optional

紧急呼叫按钮、语音交互、环境监测（烟雾/燃气/水浸）。Emergency call button, voice interaction, environmental monitoring (smoke/gas/water leak).
