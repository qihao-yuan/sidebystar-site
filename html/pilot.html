10-30 床位或 1 个楼层，验证跌倒检测、离床预警、事件闭环处置能力。10-30 beds or 1 floor, validating fall detection, bed-leaving alerts, and event loop response.

智能家居试点Smart Home Pilot

1-3 个房间，验证环境感知、设备联动、自适应场景能力。1-3 rooms, validating environmental sensing, device orchestration, and adaptive scenarios.

智能房间试点Smart Room Pilot

单一标准房间，验证多模态中枢、本地决策、空间协同的全链路能力。Single standard room, validating multimodal hub, local decision-making, and spatial coordination.
